THE PROBLEM

Africa only has five percent of the global healthcare workforce and spends just 1 percent of the global healthcare expenses. Over the next 30 years Africa’s population will balloon to two billion people and boast a larger workforce than that of china. What this means is that in order to sustain our economic growth, we must ensure that these people have easy access to world-class care. The problem is, given the length of time it takes to train a doctor, we are unlikely to train doctors fast enough to meet demand and can’t afford the cost burden of having too many sick people in hospitals. Therefore the challenge for Africa will be to devise innovative solutions to ensure that people not only have easy access to world-class care but also to information that will help them live healthy and stay healthy because the future of Africa depends on them.

THE SOLUTION



DOCTA is a one stop app that provides you with an easy, convenient and affordable way to manage everything about your health, from getting easy access to world-class care anytime anywhere at the touch of a button to having access to key information to help you live healthy and stay healthy.

Ultimately, our vision is to create an intelligent app that will provide easy access to world-class care, enhance people’s health in all facets and revolutionize the traditional way of delivering healthcare across communities in Africa and beyond.
